PROGRAM

2nd International Workshop on Distributed Event-Based Systems (DEBS'03)
Sunday, June 8th, 2003 in San Diego, California.
In cooperation with SIGMOD.

Invited Speakers:

Invited talk - Some Open Problems in Publish/Subscribe Networking. David Rosenblum, PreCache.

Invited talk - Handling XML Messages in Volume. Adam Bosworth, BEA.

Program:

8:00 Continental Breakfast as part of FCRC/SIGMOD.
8:30- 8:35 Workshop Opening - Program Chair Hans-Arno Jacobsen
8:35- 9:25 Invited talk - Some Open Problems in Publish/Subscribe Networking. David Rosenblum, PreCache Inc.
9:25-10:00 Session 1 - A Potpourri of Ideas for Event-based Processing
(approximately 40 minutes, 5-minutes short talks and discussion)
Chair: Ludger Fiege.
Role-Based Access Control for Publish/Subscribe Middleware Architectures. Andras Belokosztolszki, David M. Eyers, Peter R. Pietzuch, Jean Bacon, Ken Moody.

A-mediAS: An Adaptive Event Notification System. Annika Hinze.

A Case Study on Event Dissemination in an Active Overlay Network Environment. Sérgio Duarte, J. Legatheaux Martins, Henrique J. Domingos, and Nuno Preguiça

An Efficient Spatial Publish/Subscribe System for Intelligent Location-Based Services. Xiaoyan Chen, Ying Chen and Fangyan Rao.
10:00-10:30 Coffee Break
10:30-11:30 Session 2 - Overlay Networks, Peer-to-Peer Computing and Event-based Middleware
(approximately 60 minutes, 15-minutes talks and discussion)
Chair: David Rosenblum.
A Peer-to-Peer Approach to Content-Based Publish/Subscribe. Wesley W. Terpstra,Stefan Behnel, Ludger Fiege, Andreas Zeidler, and Alejandro P. Buchmann.

Peer-to-Peer Overlay Broker Networks in an Event-Based Middleware. Peter R. Pietzuch and Jean Bacon.

Introducing Reliability in Content-Based Publish-Subscribe through Epidemic Algorithms. Paolo Costa, Matteo Migliavacca, Gian Pietro Picco, and Gianpaolo Cugola

11:30-12:30 Session 3 - Supporting Mobility in Publish/Subscribe Middleware
(approximately 60 minutes, 15-minutes talks and discussion)
Chair: Peter Pietzuch.
Looking into the Past: Enhancing Mobile Publish/Subscribe Middleware. M. Cilia, L. Fiege, C. Haul, A. Zeidler, and A. P. Buchmann.

Client Mobility in Rendezvous-Notify. Sasu Tarkoma, Jaakko Kangasharju and Kimmo Raatikainen.

The Design of a Configurable, Extensible and Dynamic Notification Service. Roberto Silva Filho, Cleidson R. B. de Souza, David F. Redmiles.
12:30- 2:00 Lunch
2:00- 3:00 Invited talk - Handling XML Messages in Volume. by Adam Bosworth, BEA.
3:00- 3:30 Session 4 - Matching Algorithms
(approximately 30-minutes, 10-minutes talks and discussion)
Chair: Annika Hinze.
Fast and Flexible Forwarding for Internet Subscription Systems. Joanna Kulik.

Attribute-based Filtering: Improving the Expressiveness While Keeping the Predictability in P/S Systems. Carlos Mitidieri and Joerg Kaiser.
3:30- 4:00 Coffee Break
4:00- 5:00 Session 5 - Database Issues for Event-Based Middleware
(approximately 60 minutes, 15-minutes talks + discussion)
Chair: Hans-Arno Jacobsen.
Relational Subscription Middleware for Internet-Scale Publish-Subscribe. Yuhui Jin and Rob Strom.

MJoin: A Metadata-Aware Stream Join Operator. Luping Ding, Elke A. Rundensteiner and George T. Heineman.

Information Sharing With the Oracle Database. Dieter Gawlick and Shailendra Mishra.
5:00- 5:30 Closing Panel - Distributed Event-Based Processing in a Bigger Context (A summarizing report will be made available.)
Panel: Shailendra Mishra, David Rosenblum, and Rob Strom.
Panel Chair: Hans-Arno Jacobsen. Panel Chair and Audience address the Panelists with questions.
5:30- 5:35 Closing Remarks, Program Chair Hans-Arno Jacobsen.
